Vrinda Store Sales Data Analysis (Excel Project)

📌 Project Overview

This project involves analyzing the annual sales data of a store named Vrinda. The objective was to create an annual sales report to help the store understand customer trends, enhance marketing strategies, and boost sales.

📂 Objectives

To compare sales and orders using charts.

To identify the months with the highest sales and orders.

To determine who purchased more: men or women.

To understand different order statuses.

To list the top 5 states contributing to sales.

To analyze the relationship between age and gender.

To identify which channel contributes the most to sales.

To find the highest-selling category.

📊 Tools & Techniques Used

Microsoft Excel: Pivot Tables, Charts, Conditional Formatting, Data Analysis.

🔍 Key Insights

Gender Analysis: Women are more likely to buy compared to men (~65%).

Top Contributing States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh (~35% of total sales).

Age Group Analysis: Adult age group (30-49 years) contributes the most to sales (~50%).

Sales Channels: Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the major contributing platforms (~80% of total sales).

💡 Recommendations

Target women customers aged 30-49 years living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.

Implement ads, offers, and coupons through Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra to boost sales.
